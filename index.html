About Me

Javier, a budding photographer with an affinity for capturing the depth and emotion within dark imagery, delves into his craft with fervent enthusiasm.

We can checkout his recent works and experiences below. He considers photography as his hobby.

what am i doing?

  • I'm immersing myself in the vibrant tapestry of the streets, capturing candid moments and raw emotions through street photography. I'm freezing time and encapsulating the essence of humanity in every frame.
  • And in cosplay photography, I'm bringing fictional characters to life, blending fantasy with reality to create stunning visual narratives. With intricate costumes and elaborate settings, I'm transforming ordinary moments into epic adventures.
  • In portrait photography, I'm delving into the souls of my subjects, revealing their stories and personalities through the lens. Each portrait is a window into a unique world, a reflection of the individual's innermost self.
  • In all these endeavors, I'm not just taking pictures; I'm creating art, preserving memories, and sharing glimpses of the world as I see it. So, what am I doing? I'm capturing moments, crafting stories, and painting with light.
